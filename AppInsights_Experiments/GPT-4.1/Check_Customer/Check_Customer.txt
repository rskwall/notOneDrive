# LegaSuite DEMO Application Documentation

## Overview

The LegaSuite DEMO application is a demonstration tool designed to showcase the capabilities of LegaSuite products on IBM i systems. It is not intended for real-time or production use, but rather to illustrate various features and navigation patterns available within LegaSuite-enabled applications.

## Main Features

- **Customer Maintenance:** View, add, update, or delete customer records.
- **Customer Payments:** Manage and review payments made by customers.
- **Item Maintenance:** Maintain inventory items.
- **Incoming Stock:** Track and manage incoming stock.
- **Order Entry:** Enter and manage customer orders.
- **Delivered Orders:** Review orders that have been delivered.
- **View Turnover:** View customer and item turnover statistics.
- **End of Year Procedure:** Perform end-of-year processing.
- **Sign Off:** Exit the application.

## Menu Navigation

### Main Menu

Upon logging in, you are presented with the main menu:

```
Select one of the following:
  1. Maintain Customers
  2. Customer Payments
  3. Maintain Items
  4. Incoming Stock
  6. Order Entry
  7. Delivered Orders
  9. View Customer Turn over
 10. View Item Turn over
 80. End of year procedure
 90. Sign off
```

- To select an option, type the corresponding number at the `===>` prompt and press **Enter**.

### Example Navigation: Maintain Customers

1. **Select "1"** at the main menu to access Customer Maintenance.
2. You will be prompted for a Customer Number. You can:
   - Enter a customer number and press **Enter** to view or edit details.
   - Press **F4** to prompt for a customer number.
   - Press **F6** to add a new customer.
   - Press **F14** to search by customer name.
3. When searching or prompting, a list of customers is displayed. Use:
   - **RollUp** (Page Down) to scroll through the list.
   - **Enter** on a customer to select and view details.
4. In the customer detail screen, you can:
   - View and edit customer information.
   - Press **F3** to exit.
   - Press **F12** to cancel.
   - Press **F23** to delete the customer.

### Returning to the Main Menu

- Use **F3** or **F12** to exit submenus and return to the main menu.
- To sign off, enter `90` at the main menu prompt and press **Enter**.

## Function Key Reference

- **F3**: Exit
- **F4**: Prompt
- **F6**: Add (where available)
- **F7**: Fold/Unfold (in lists)
- **F12**: Cancel
- **F14**: Search Name (in Customer Maintenance)
- **F23**: Delete (in Customer Maintenance)
