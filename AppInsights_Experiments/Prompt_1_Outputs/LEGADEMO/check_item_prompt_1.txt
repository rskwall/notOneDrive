**Pre-condition:** You are at the LEGADEMO main menu (screen title: *LegaSuite DEMO*).

> **Note:** If you are at the IBM i Main Menu, type `legademo` at the `===>` prompt and press **Enter**, then press **Enter** again to dismiss the Introduction screen.

---

### Steps

**1.** `[Constant]` At the `===>` prompt, type `3` and press **Enter**.
- Selects *Maintain Items* — the Item Number entry screen appears.

**2.** `[Constant]` Press **F4** (Prompt).
- A *Select Item* popup window opens, listing all available items by number and description.

**3.** `[Optional]` Review the item list. Press **Page Down** to scroll if the list has additional entries.

**4.** `[Constant]` Press **F3** (Exit) to close the Select Item window.
- Returns directly to the LEGADEMO main menu.

**5.** `[Constant]` At the `===>` prompt, type `3` and press **Enter**.
- Returns to the Item Number entry screen.

**6.** `[Variable]` In the *Item Number* field, type `[Item Number]` and press **Enter**.
- The item detail screen opens, displaying the item's current description, price, quantity in stock, and year-to-date turnover.

**7.** `[Variable]` In the *Item Price* field, overtype the current value with `[New Item Price]` and press **Enter**.
- The updated price is saved. The Item Number entry screen is displayed again.

**8.** `[Variable]` In the *Item Number* field, type `[Item Number]` (the same number used in step 6) and press **Enter**.
- The item detail screen reopens. **Verify** that the *Item Price* field now shows the value entered in step 7.

**9.** `[Constant]` Press **F3** (Exit).
- Returns to the LEGADEMO main menu.

**10.** `[Constant]` At the `===>` prompt, type `90` and press **Enter**.
- Signs off from the application.

---

### Placeholders

| Placeholder | What to supply |
|---|---|
| `[Item Number]` | The numeric identifier of the item you want to view or update |
| `[New Item Price]` | The new price to assign to the item (numeric, decimal format) |
