

**Starting point:** The LEGADEMO main menu (`===>` prompt) is displayed.

**Common pattern – Selecting from a popup list:** When a selection list appears, scroll to locate the desired row. Type `1` in the selection column next to the target row and press **Enter**.

---

## Sub-Workflow 1: Maintain Customers – View and Optionally Update a Customer Record

1. At `===>`, type `1` and press **Enter**. *(Maintain Customers – CUSMAIN-F000)*
2. Press **F4** (Prompt) to open the customer selection list. *(Select Customer popup)*
3. Type `1` next to **[Target Customer]** and press **Enter**. *(Customer detail – CUSMAIN-F100)*
4. *(Optional – to edit a field)* Tab to **[Field to Update]**, overtype the current value with **[New Field Value]**, then press **Enter** to save. *(Returns to CUSMAIN-F000)*
5. Press **F3** (Exit) to return to the LEGADEMO main menu.

## Sub-Workflow 7: View Customer Turn Over – Display a Customer Turn Over Report

1. At `===>`, type `9` and press **Enter**. *(Turn Over per Customer – TRNCUST-F000)*
2. With the cursor on the "From Customer number" field, press **F4** (Prompt).
3. Type `1` next to **[Starting Customer]** and press **Enter**. *(Field is now populated)*
4. Tab to the "To Customer number" field and press **F4** (Prompt).
5. Type `1` next to **[Ending Customer]** and press **Enter**. *(Field is now populated)*
6. *(Optional)* In "Display or Print", type **[Output Mode]** — `D` = Display (default), `P` = Print.
7. *(Optional)* In "Select year", type **[Year Selection]** — `0` = This year (default), `1` = Last year, `2` = Two years ago, `3` = Three years ago.
8. Press **Enter** to generate the report. *(Overview Customer Turn over – TRNCUST-F130)*
9. Press **F3** (Exit) to return to the LEGADEMO main menu.
