### Steps

| # | Type | Action |
|---|------|--------|
| 1 | **Constant** | Type `1`, press **Enter** — selects "Maintain Customers" from the main menu |
| 2 | **Constant** | Press **F4** — opens the "Select Customer" lookup window (list of customers by number, name, and city) |

### Notes on Variable Steps

| `[Customer Number]` | The numeric ID for the customer you want to view, either noted from the browse list in steps 2–4 or known in advance |
