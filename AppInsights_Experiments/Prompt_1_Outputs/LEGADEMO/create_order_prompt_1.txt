## Create Order Workflow — Starting from the LEGADEMO Main Menu

---

### Open Order Entry

1. Type **"6"** and press **Enter**.

---

### Initiate a New Order

2. In the **Order Number** field, type **[Order Number]**.
3. Press **F6** (Add).

---

### Assign a Customer to the Order

4. In the **Customer Number** field, type **[Customer Number]** and press **Enter**.

---

### Add Order Line Items
*(Repeat steps 5–8 for each item; proceed to step 9 when all items are entered.)*

5. In the **Item Number** field on the next available blank line, type **[Item Number]**.
6. In the **Ordered Quantity** field on the same line, type **[Ordered Quantity]**.
7. In the **% Discount** field on the same line, type **[Discount Percentage]** (type **"0"** for no discount).
8. Press **Enter** to confirm the line and move to the next.

---

### Save and Complete the Order

9. Press **Enter** without entering any new item data to save the order and return to the Order Entry start screen.

---

### Verify the Order (Optional)

10. Press **F4** to open the order selection list.
11. If the new order is not yet visible, press **RollUp** (Page Down) to scroll through the list.
12. Press **F3** to close the list and exit Order Entry, returning to the LEGADEMO main menu.

---

### Sign Off (Optional)

13. Type **"90"** and press **Enter**.

---
