This is a **classic IBM i (AS/400) green‑screen business demo application** called **LegaSuite DEMO**. It is designed to **show how real business processes can be built on IBM i using LegaSuite tooling**, not to be a production system.

Below is a **plain‑English explanation of what this application is, how it works, and what you just did in it**.

***

# What This Application Is

**LegaSuite DEMO** is a **menu‑driven ERP‑style application** running on **IBM i** using **5250 terminal screens**.

Its purpose is to demonstrate:

*   Order processing
*   Customer management
*   Item management
*   Payments and deliveries
*   End‑to‑end business flows  
    all within a traditional IBM i environment.

It mimics what many real companies still use today for **back‑office operations**.

***

# How the Application Is Structured

## 1. Platform

*   **System**: IBM i (AS/400)
*   **UI**: 5250 green‑screen terminals
*   **Navigation**: Numeric menus and function keys (F‑keys)
*   **Data**: Customer, order, item, and financial records

***

## 2. Main Menu (LEGADEMO)

After launching the application (`legademo`), you land on this menu:

    1. Maintain Customers
    2. Customer Payments
    3. Maintain Items
    4. Incoming Stock
    6. Order Entry
    7. Delivered Orders
    9. View Customer Turn over
    10.View Item Turn over
    90.Sign off

Each option opens a **business module**.
